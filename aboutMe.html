About Me

And when it's time to unwind, you'll often find me immersed in the vibrant world of video games and coding. I greatly enjoy exploring new adventures and challenges. Professional Background

I am on an exciting journey towards becoming a web developer. My career path has been diverse: from honing customer service skills in sales, mastering precision and creativity as a restaurant cook, to managing logistics as a forklift driver. I've also delved into specialized roles in vinegar and zeolite production, and operated as a calciner, where I developed a keen understanding of technical processes. This rich tapestry of experiences has equipped me with a unique skill set, perfectly aligning with the dynamic and innovative world of software engineering. Ready for new challenges, I'm eager to bring my adaptable skills and fresh perspectives to the tech industry.
